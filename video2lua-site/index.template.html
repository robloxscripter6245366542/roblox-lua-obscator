Sound is a two-step flow. Roblox can only play audio from an uploaded asset, so:
  1. Use Extract audio (.wav) to save the soundtrack.
  2. Upload it to Roblox (Creator Dashboard → Audio) and copy its id.
  3. Put the id in Audio asset id above and convert again.
Without an id the video plays silently — the picture is always full quality.
